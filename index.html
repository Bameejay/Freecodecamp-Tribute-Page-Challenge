Dora Akinyuli

The Ex Director-general of National Agency for Food and Drug Administration and Control of Nigeria

Dora Akinyuli Early Life, Education and Award lifetime is summarized here.

  • 1954 - Dora Nkem Akinyuli was born in Makurdi, Benue State, Nigeria
  • 1966 - She received her First School Leaving Certificate from St. Patrick's Primary School, Isuofia, Anambra State
  • 1973 - She had her West African School Certificate Examination at Queen of the Rosary Secondary School Nsukka, Enugu State
  • 2001 - She was the director-general of National Agency for Food and Drug Administration and Control of Nigeria
  • 2005 - Most Inovative Director Award Federal Government College, Ijanikin, Lagos, October
  • 2005 - Grassroots Human Right Campaigner Award London-based Human Rights Defense Organization
  • 2005 - An Icon of Excellence Award - The African Cultural Institute and Zenith Bank
  • 2005 - Award for the Best Government Parastatal
  • 2005 - African Virtuous and Entrepreneurial Women Merit Award
  • 2005 - Award of Excellence - Advocacy for Democracy Dividends International, Lagos
  • 2005 - Award of Excellence - Integrated World Services (IWS)
  • 2006 - Person of the Year 2005 Award - Silverbird Communications Ltd
  • 2006 - Time magazine award
  • 2014 - Dies at the age of 59.
